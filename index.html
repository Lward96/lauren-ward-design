The girl
behind the code

Hi, I'm Lauren!

I'm a graphic designer turned web designer with a passion for helping my clients land a design they love. Although I live and work in New York and Berlin, I enjoy helping clients all over the world
get noticed online.

Website Design & Development

Combining an eye for design and web-development skills, I create web pages that are beautiful and technically sound. It's important to me that you are happy with your design--I'll go the distance to make sure you love the way your website looks. My webpages include forms, animations, and can integrate e-commerce platforms. 

Mobile friendly
Web Design

Most modern websites are viewed on cell phones, which makes responsive, mobile-first websites the best choice. My designs adapt to different screen sizes, ensuring that your viewers will always be able to view your website, regardless of the device they are using. 

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

When it comes to SEO,
content is king.

If you want your website to be more visible online, let me code with SEO in mind and produce content on your website that will boost traffic.

you will get:

Articles that attract interest and show how knowledgeable you are.

Images that catch the eye and are titled for optimal SEO.

Compeditive
prices

I host websites for free though GitHub, which helps lower your annual costs by between $50-$180 per year. Options like Wix and Squarespace cost around $200-$500 per year, which can certainly add up over time. I offer competitive pricing that will save you money in the long run.

Website Maintenance

All websites change over time. You may need to add articles, update information about your business, or upload your latest painting. I'm here to help when you need it.
